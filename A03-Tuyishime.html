About Mad Mask

Mad Mask's goal is to make an online mask warehouse with custom masks that cannot be obtained anywhere else. These masks will be special in many regards. The main focuses are price, quality and of course fashion. Additionally we understand that expression is limited so we would like customers' ideas to come to the forefront therefore we will allow users to submit their own designs to be printed on mask and delivered. This is because at Mad Mask’s while we make mad masks we only want happy customers.

Our Mission

Due to the pandemic, the future workplace will never be the same as everyone will be required to wear a mask. As we care about the safety and the sense style, our maks provide a premium safety recommended by health experts and the chance to express self through customized design. Mad Mask’s masks are manufactured using flexible clothing that allow the customer to breath without difficulty. Unlike most mask manufacturing companies, our masks are available in all size, color, and cloth quality. Additionally, the trademark of our distinct mask is the innovation of scenting masks. This can be in the forms of mint, strawberry,cherry and many more. This innovation in mask design will lead to whole levels of comfort in using a mask as the experience will always be filled with a pleasing scent. This together with our astonishing designs will lead many to desire quality masks we provide.
